Creative Director/Front-End Developer

 

Creating a user interface it's just not about coding or designing a page, but to think from user point-of-view of what information they're looking for and how easily accessible when interacting with a system. With a deeper understanding of user interest plus advance knowledge in creative visualisation I can make the best possible environment for presenting the information. Methods, technologies and tools to make such an environment may change in every single project, but the core ideology while designing / developing a perfect interface will remain focused in maximizing Accessibiliy, Usability and Controllability .
